# Countpluse Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Countpluse respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information
the app may collect, how it is used, and what choices users have.

## Information We Collect

Countpluse may collect and process the following information:

- account information such as name, email address, and phone number when you
  register or log in
- one-time password (OTP) verification data used for authentication
- devotional counting data such as daily counts and summary information
- optional profile photo and optional wallpaper selected by the user
- microphone input only when the user actively uses the voice-counting feature

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- create and verify user access
- enable guest or logged-in usage
- support devotional counting and summary features
- personalize the app experience with optional profile and wallpaper features
- improve reliability, support, and security

## Camera and Microphone Access

Countpluse requests access only for app features selected by the user:

- Camera: used only when the user chooses to capture a profile photo or home
  wallpaper
- Microphone: used only when the user enables voice-assisted counting

These permissions are optional for general app use.

## Data Sharing

Countpluse does not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service
providers that support authentication, email delivery, backend hosting, and
database infrastructure.

## Data Retention

Data retention depends on the app configuration and backend environment in use.
Guest-mode and session-only experiences may retain less data than authenticated
server-backed flows.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data, but no method of storage or
transmission over the internet is completely secure.

## Children

Countpluse is not directed toward children under 13.

## Your Choices

You may choose not to provide optional data such as profile photo, wallpaper,
or microphone access. You may also stop using the app at any time.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, use the support contact you
provide in the Play Store listing.
