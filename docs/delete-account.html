Account

Request account deletion

Japlo users can request deletion of their account and associated account-backed data by contacting our support team.

Email deletion request

How to request deletion

  1. Email japlosupport@gmail.com with the subject line Delete my Japlo account.
  2. Send the request from the email address linked to your Japlo account, or include your registered email address or phone number in the message.
  3. We may reply to confirm the request before deleting account data.

What will be deleted

  • Account profile information linked to your Japlo account, such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Account-backed devotional count history and related summary data stored for your account.
  • Optional account-linked profile photo or wallpaper data stored with your account, if applicable.

What may be retained temporarily

Limited records may be retained for a short period when reasonably needed for security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or legal compliance. Any such retained information is kept only for as long as necessary for those purposes.

Timeline

We aim to process valid deletion requests within 7 business days.

Questions

If you have questions about account deletion, contact japlosupport@gmail.com.
