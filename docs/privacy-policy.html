Countpluse

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Countpluse respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and what choices users have.

Information We Collect

Countpluse may collect and process the following information:

  • Account information such as name, email address, and phone number when you register or log in.
  • One-time password (OTP) verification data used for authentication.
  • Devotional counting data such as daily counts and summary information.
  • Optional profile photo and optional wallpaper selected by the user.
  • Microphone input only when the user actively uses the voice-counting feature.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and verify user access.
  • Enable guest or logged-in usage.
  • Support devotional counting and summary features.
  • Personalize the app experience with optional profile and wallpaper features.
  • Improve reliability, support, and security.

Camera and Microphone Access

Countpluse requests access only for app features selected by the user:

  • Camera: used only when the user chooses to capture a profile photo or home wallpaper.
  • Microphone: used only when the user enables voice-assisted counting.

These permissions are optional for general app use.

Data Sharing

Countpluse does not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers that support authentication, email delivery, backend hosting, and database infrastructure.

Data Retention

Data retention depends on the app configuration and backend environment in use. Guest-mode and session-only experiences may retain less data than authenticated server-backed flows.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data, but no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children

Countpluse is not directed toward children under 13.

Your Choices

You may choose not to provide optional data such as profile photo, wallpaper, or microphone access. You may also stop using the app at any time.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, use the support contact you provide in the Play Store listing.
